Information

This program is designed by soccer players for anyone who wants to improve his or her game. Our goal is to improve performance through private soccer lessons with specialized training in order for players to gain consistent performance. We guide our athletes with a goal setting system to help them achieve their dreams.10X Soccer Performance© provides the methods in which soccer players can identify and work toward quantifiable improvements both individually and with their team.

How it works

We start by doing an evaluation that involves us taking a close look at the technical, mental, physical and tactical aspects of the player. We will use the information gathered during the assessment to design an individualized training program. As a result, a training plan will be provided on the second training. The player will be closely coached through the entirety of the program by our team of coaches.
